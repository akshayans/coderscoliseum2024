Ocean Wise

Ocean Wise is an organization that wants to protect and restore oceans across the globe.

They say they have three ways to be "Ocean Wise".

You can learn about plastic pollution, join a Shoreline Cleanup, or donate.

Ocean Conservancy

Ocean Conservancy is a non-profit organization to help protect the ocean.

They make solutions for a healthy ocean and the wildlife/communities that depend on it.

Trees For The Future

Trees For The Future is ending hunger, poverty and environmental destruction.

They restore landscapes in developing communities.

Arbor Day Foundation

The Arbor Day Foundation is on a mission to plant millions of trees.

They've planted more than 500 million trees across more than 50 countries.
